About Us

Connecting Worlds with Power & Technology.

About Us

Ndawonye was founded in 2020 by a group of young professionals (engineers, electricians, and technicians) who understand the dynamics, processes, and solutions in ICT, ElectroniX, and electrical fields. We are a 100% black-owned and managed ICT and Electrical Company, focused on maximizing clients' ROI and providing robust solutions.

We have a clear understanding of health, safety, and environmental issues related to our work, including required permits and licenses. Our technical staff are highly experienced engineers and technicians committed to BBBEE.

Mission

To be the ICT, ElectroniX, and Electrical Company of choice for leading service providers, government agencies, and major enterprises by providing quality services and timely solutions through professional delivery, including enterprise networking, business intelligence, electronics, electrical solutions, and ICT consultancy.

Vision

To be the company of choice in providing ElectroniX, Electrical, and ICT services through operational excellence, innovative products, and long-term partnerships with clients.

What We Do

Ndawonye provides comprehensive ICT, ElectroniX, and Electrical solutions for businesses, government agencies, and enterprises. We combine innovation, expertise, and modern technology to deliver reliable and scalable solutions.

ICT Solutions

Enterprise networks, wireless solutions, software, website design/hosting, and business intelligence services.

Networking

Structured cabling, data centers, WAN/WLAN/PTP/PTMP, microwave links, optic fiber networking, VoIP, and PABX solutions.

Electrical & ElectroniX

Generators, inverters, UPS, HV/LV reticulations, armature winders, lighting, CCTV, security, AV/multimedia, and electrical systems.

Hardware & Devices

Printers, POS systems, scanners, barcoding infrastructure, ATMs, telecommunication devices, and office hardware solutions.

Maintenance & Repairs

Repairs and maintenance for ICT equipment, electrical systems, networking devices, and electronics hardware.

Consultancy

ICT, ElectroniX, and Electrical consultancy services for enterprise management, security systems, and strategic infrastructure planning.
